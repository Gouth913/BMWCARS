2022 BMW M3 Sedan Overview

Under the hood of the M3 is a turbocharged inline-six-cylinder engine that makes 473 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. This motor and tune can be paired with a rear-wheel-drive and a six-speed manual transmission, which enables a 0-100 km/h sprint of 4.2 seconds. The automatic model is known as the M3 Competition, which bumps performance up to 503 horsepower and 379 lb-ft of torque. This model can sprint to 100 km/h in just 3.9 seconds. The Competition model can also be equipped with xDrive all-wheel-drive.
